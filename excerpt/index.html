1: Prepare an appropriate amount of flour, panko, cooking wine, soy sauce,
soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper, salt, sugar, MSG, sesame oil and green onion.
2: Pour the flour into a large bowl, pour warm water into it in batches and stir,
and knead it into a smooth dough with your hands. Moisten a drawer cloth to cover the dough for half an hour.
3: Cut the pancetta into minced meat, put it into a large bowl, pour in the wine,
soy sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper, salt, sugar, MSG and sesame oil, mix well and marinate for 20 minutes.
4: Take the white onion and cut it into pieces of the same size as the minced meat,
pour it into the meat mixture and mix well.
